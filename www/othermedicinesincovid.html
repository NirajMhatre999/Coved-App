ROLE OF OTHER MEDICINES IN COVID

Hello Guys! There are many medicines which are used in the treatment of covid so here we are going to see some of those medicines and will check if they are effective or not. All of this information is just for your understanding so please don't use these medicines by yourself.

DEXAMETHASONE

It is a steroid which is very cheap and is used in general practise. Steroids are already available in our body. The role of steroids is to regulate electrolytes and to help in our growth. Dexamethasone is one such steroid which is known as Glucocorticoid. It is used in treatment of asthama, COPD, rheumetoid arthritis and even cancer. In ARDS when imflammation occurs then it is used as an anti-inflammatory. However, due to the fact that some viruses affect rapidly with use of steroids this was used at a very feeble amount so as to prevent any fatal effect.

LET'S SEE A STUDY

A study was carried out by Oxford University to test the effectiveness of a low dose of Dexamethasone. In this trial people were divided into two groups. One group was administered with low dose of dexamethasone and another group was given the regular treatment. After 28 days the results were analysed. The results showed that those who don't need oxygen had no use of the dose. Those who required oxygen about 1/25th of the people had benefit from the dose while those who were on ventilator 1/8th of people showed positive effects. However, this should be only used for those who have severe Covid.

REMDESIVIR

It is an antiviral medicine. A study was done in USA wherein people were divided into two groups based on the oxygen requirement. The results showed that those who required oxygen showed a fast recovery but those who require ventilator or high flow nasal system it doesn't show any effect on those.

AVIFAVIR

It was the first drug which recieved approval in China then it was used by several other nations. It shows a positive effect in the recovery and the transmission level is lowered. Still it isn't much effective in reducing mortality rate.

TRIPLE DRUG THERAPY

In this therapy three antiviral medications are administered wherein two are used in HIV and are used with interferon beta-1b which shows a faster recovery rate but this hasn't been tested much on the severe or critical patients much.

CYCLESONIDE

It is originally used in asthama by means of inhaler but it has been observed to be effective in reducing the replication of viruses and to reduce inflammation. It is currently in trial but any results haven't been shared.

CONVALSCENT PLASMA

It is a simple technique which is known as passive immunization. In this technique the person who has recovered from Covid their plasma is given to those who are infected by covid so the antibodies present in the plasma help in countering covid.

TOCILIZUMAB(TCZ)

It is an immunological medicine used to supress the immunity of patients. In covid patients sometimes the condition known as cytokine storm occurs which affects each and every organ of the body so it is used to supress the hyperactivity of immunity in such cases.

IVERMECTIN + DOXYCYCLINE

A study was conducted in bangladesh where this combination was administered to people infected with covid and claims are made that it is very effective in treatment. However, this can only be concluded when the mass trails are done.

AZITHROMYCIN

It is an antiviral drug. It was claimed to be effective in treatment of covid but the trials resulted in contradictory results wherein it led to heart conditions in some cases.

LMWH

Low Molecular Weight Heparin is used in treatment of some patients wherein blood clots begin to form in the body and these clots can lead to harmful effects so this drug is used to prevent the formation of blood clots.

PARACETAMOL OR IBUPROFEN

These are used for controlling the symptoms of Covid patients. However, these aren't administered to all the patients since these can cause complications.

INSULIN

People with disturbed sugar levels are more vernuable to Covid and possess a higher fatality rate so insulin is given to such patients to regulate their blood sugar level.
