LIFESTYLE DURING COVID

Hello guys from the start of this pandemic people have started to be very conscious about their health and Lifestyle so here we are going to cover all of the topics related to it this is because these things are going to help us even after the pandemic.

LIFESTYLE PROBLEMS

We can see that many people these days are suffering from heart problems blood pressure diabetes respiratory disorders obesity and many such disorders so inculcating these habits May help us in countering these problems there are many such Lifestyle practices and I will try to include some of them.

LET'S SEE ALL THESE FACTORS

There are mainly 5 factors which affect our Lifestyle and health they are as follows:-
1. Physical factors
2. Mental factors
3. Social factors
4. Environment factors
5. Intellectual factors

1. PHYSICAL FACTORS

These factors are very crucial in maintaining a proper physique we can use some of the methods to find if we are in an ideal physical condition.

I. BODY MASS INDEX(BMI)

Moving to the first one we have body mass index BMI it is a factor which helps in determining the ideal weight suited for specific height and weight of an individual it is a scale ranging from underweight Tu extreme obese categories depending on the BMI calculation for having ideal weight for the specified age group the BMI I should be in the range of 18.5 Tu 24.9 those who have a BMI less than 18.5 the fall under the category of underweight and those over BMI of 24.9 are are either overweight Or obese depending on their BMI value so calculating BMI is a very e simple and efficient way of having a check on your fitness routine.

II. FRAILTY

The second method which is used to check fitness is the method of frailty this has been divided into 9 categories from very fit to Terminal ill. Those people who fall under the very fit category are generally active and perform exercises on a daily routine whereas as we move down through this list we have the people in the terminal illness category who are suffering from the disease and are very very week.

2. MENTAL FACTORS

These factors are very important in maintaining the mental state it is affected by sleep anxiety and other such factors.

3. SOCIAL FACTORS

Social factors are some of the major factors which affect our Lifestyle. One of the biggest reason being the social media addiction which has a collective effect on our health such as it can affect physically by causing eye strain and Blurred vision also so it can have mental effects such as depression loneliness fear and even suicidal thoughts.

4. ENVIRONMENTAL FACTORS

Every environment is different it and the people living there have to adapt to the lifestyle which best suits an environment.

5. INTELLECTUAL FACTORS

This factor comes into play when people share ideologies contrary to each other which creates differences and a feeling of hatred among them.
