DOES COVID SPREAD THROUGH AIR?

A global body of scientists presented a proposal to the WORLD HEALTH ORGANIZATION stating that the CORONA VIRUS is spreading through the air and should provide necessary guidance. Let's find that if the studies back this claim.

LET'S FIND OUT HOW IT STARTED

So When the corona outbreak took place in Wuhan, China the doctors there carried out a survey wherein they studied 100000-125000 people closely where they concluded that this spreads through droplets.

DIFFERENCE BETWEEN TRANSMISSION THROUGH AIR AND DROPLETS

So there is a huge difference between transmission through the air and the transmission through droplets. Transmission through air suggests that the virus is suspended into the air and then is carried by the airflow whereas. Transmission through droplets means that the virus is present in the droplets that are expelled by the people when anyone who is infected by COVID either coughs or sneezes in the open and they travel through the air and whenever they come in contact with the active sights such as mouth, nose or eyes the transmission takes place.

LET'S SEE WHAT ACTUALLY HAPPENS

If we consider the coronavirus then, it spreads through droplets and even microdroplets which are extremely small droplets capable of covering large distances.

LET'S SEE A STUDY

New England journal of medicine published the observations of an experiment wherein they tried to recreate the droplets using machinery and it was observed that microdroplets that are less than 10 microns in size were found to be suspended in the air for about 3 hours..

CONSIDER SOME POINTS HERE

These were obvious results obtained by the machinery so there is a chance of probable error because there is always a difference between actual results and in machine operated clinical results. For Example, Hydroxychloroquine which was found to be effective against the virus during clinical tests doesn't provide much satisfactory results in practical use as expected. Also, scientists believe that the viral load carried by such microdroplets isn't enough to cause transmission.

FACTORS AFFECTING DROPLETS

Another point to note is that the availability of these droplets in air depends on various factors such as the place, airflow, and many more.

1. PLACE

If we talk about the place then we find that if the droplets are present in a closed place then the droplets are suspended in the air for a greater period of time as compared to open places because in the case of open places the droplets are displaced from their mean position.

2. AIRFLOW

Another factor is the airflow because places with higher airflow displace the droplets more while places with low airflow cause the droplets to settle faster.

CONCLUSION

So currently there is no clinical proof that proves that the transmission of COVID takes place directly through the air. Hence, WHO hasn't changed its stand from the fact that COVID is transmitted through droplets. So everyone is suggested to maintain personal hygiene and to use safety gear to prevent the transmission through droplets.
